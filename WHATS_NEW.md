## 📱 How to Use New Features

### For Donors:

**View NGO Details:**
1. Create a donation
2. Wait for NGO to accept
3. Go to "Track Donations"
4. Select your donation
5. Scroll down to see NGO Details card
6. ✅ See complete NGO information!

**What You'll See:**
- NGO contact person
- Phone number to call
- Complete address
- Registration credentials
- Verification status

---

### For NGOs:

**View Donor Details:**
1. Go to "Requests" page
2. Browse available donations
3. ✅ See donor phone & address on each card
4. Accept donation
5. Go to "My Accepted" tab
6. Click "View Tracking"
7. ✅ See all donor details in modal

**What You'll See:**
- Donor phone number
- Complete pickup address
- Donor type (context)
- All contact information

**Update Status:**
1. Accept donation
2. Click "Mark as Picked Up" 📦
3. Click "Mark In Transit" 🚚
4. Click "Mark as Delivered" ✅
5. ✅ Donor receives notifications!
