# 🏥 How to Get Your NGO Verified - Step by Step Guide

## For NGOs: How to Apply for Verification

### ✅ Step 1: Register as NGO

1. Go to the registration page: `http://localhost:3000/register`
2. Fill in basic information:
   - Name
   - Email
   - Password
3. **Select Role: "🏥 NGO - I want to collect donations"**

### ✅ Step 2: Fill Verification Details

When you select NGO role, additional fields will appear. Fill ALL mandatory fields:

**Mandatory Fields** (marked with *):
- **Registration Number*** - Your government registration number (e.g., MH/2020/123456)
- **Registration Type*** - Select one:
  - Trust Act
  - Society Registration Act
  - Section 8 Company
- **Registered Address*** - Your NGO's official registered address
- **City*** - City where NGO is registered
- **State*** - State where NGO is registered
- **Contact Person Name*** - Name of primary contact person
- **Contact Phone*** - Contact phone number (e.g., +91 1234567890)

**Optional Fields**:
- GST Number - If you have GST registration
- PAN Number - Your NGO's PAN
- Website - Your NGO's website URL

### ✅ Step 3: Submit Registration

1. Click "Create Account" button
2. You'll see success message: **"NGO registered successfully. Your account is pending verification by admin."**
3. Your verification application is automatically submitted!

### ✅ Step 4: Wait for Admin Approval

**What happens now:**
- Your account is created with status: **"Pending Verification"**
- All admins are automatically notified about your registration
- Admin will review your details (typically 24-48 hours)
- You'll receive a notification when verified

### ✅ Step 5: Login and Check Status

1. Login with your email and password
2. You'll be redirected to NGO Dashboard
3. You'll see a **yellow banner** at the top:

```
⏳ Verification Pending

Your NGO account is currently under review by our admin team. 
You cannot accept food donations until your account is verified.

📋 What happens next:
• Admin will review your registration details
• Verification typically takes 24-48 hours
• You'll be notified once verified
• After verification, you can accept donations

💡 You can browse available donations but cannot accept them yet
```

### ✅ Step 6: While Waiting

**What you CAN do:**
- ✅ Browse available food donations
- ✅ See donation details
- ✅ View your dashboard
- ✅ Check nearby donations with distance

**What you CANNOT do:**
- ❌ Accept donations (buttons are disabled)
- ❌ Update donation status
- ❌ Receive donation assignments

### ✅ Step 7: After Verification

**If APPROVED:**
1. You'll receive a notification: **"🎉 Your NGO has been verified! You can now accept food donations."**
2. Login to see **green banner**:
```
✅ Verified NGO
Your NGO is verified and can accept food donations
```
3. All "Accept Donation" buttons are now enabled
4. You can start accepting and managing donations!

**If REJECTED:**
1. You'll receive notification with rejection reason
2. Login to see **red banner** with reason
3. Contact admin to resolve issues
4. Re-apply if needed

## For Admins: How to Verify NGOs

### ✅ Step 1: Receive Notification

When an NGO registers, you'll automatically receive a notification:
```
New NGO registration: [NGO Name] - Pending verification
```

### ✅ Step 2: Go to Verification Page

1. Login as admin
2. Click **"Verify NGOs"** in the sidebar
3. You'll see all pending NGO registrations

### ✅ Step 3: Review NGO Details

Each NGO card shows:
- NGO Name and Email
- Registration Number
- Registration Type
- Registered Address
- City and State
- Contact Person and Phone
- GST Number (if provided)
- PAN Number (if provided)
- Website (if provided)
- Registration Date

### ✅ Step 4: Make Decision

**To APPROVE:**
1. Click green **"✓ Approve"** button
2. NGO is immediately verified
3. NGO receives notification
4. NGO can now accept donations

**To REJECT:**
1. Click red **"✗ Reject"** button
2. Text area appears
3. Enter rejection reason (e.g., "Invalid registration number")
4. Click **"Confirm Rejection"**
5. NGO receives notification with reason

When reviewing NGO applications, verify:

- [ ] Registration number is valid format

- [ ] Address is complete and valid
- [ ] Contact information is reachable
- [ ] GST/PAN numbers are valid (if provided)

## 📊 Verification Status Explained

### 🟡 Pending (Default)
- **When**: Immediately after NGO registration
- **NGO Can**: Browse donations, view dashboard
- **NGO Cannot**: Accept donations
- **Action Needed**: Admin must review and approve/reject

### 🟢 Verified
- **When**: After admin approves
- **NGO Can**: Accept donations, update status, full access
- **NGO Cannot**: Nothing restricted
- **Action Needed**: None - fully operational

### 🔴 Rejected
- **When**: After admin rejects with reason
- **NGO Can**: Login and see rejection reason
- **NGO Cannot**: Accept donations, any NGO functions
- **Action Needed**: Contact admin or re-register

## 🚨 Common Issues & Solutions

### Issue 1: "I registered but don't see verification fields"
**Solution**: Make sure you selected "NGO" role during registration. If you selected "Donor" by mistake, register again with NGO role.

### Issue 2: "I can't accept donations"
**Solution**: Check the banner on your dashboard. If it says "Pending Verification", wait for admin approval. If "Rejected", contact admin.

### Issue 3: "How long does verification take?"
**Solution**: Typically 24-48 hours. Admin receives instant notification when you register.

### Issue 4: "I was rejected, what now?"
**Solution**: Login to see the rejection reason. Contact admin to resolve issues. You may need to re-register with correct information.

### Issue 5: "Can I update my verification details?"
**Solution**: Currently, verification details are set during registration. Contact admin if you need to update information.
